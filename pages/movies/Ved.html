synopsis
Ved ( transl. Madness) is a 2022 Indian Marathi-language romantic drama film directed by Riteish Deshmukh, in his directorial debut, and produced by Genelia D'Souza, both of whom also star. Ashok Saraf and Jiya Shankar play supporting roles. It is a remake of the 2019 Telugu-language romantic drama, Majili.
